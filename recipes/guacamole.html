Guacamole

Description

Restaurant-worthy guacamole is just a few ingredients away. You'll be surprised how easy it is to make this top-rated guacamole recipe. You'll come back to it again and again! (Taken from www.allrecipes.com)

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
